About Nishu Yoga

Nishu is a passionate yoga player who started practicing yoga 2 years ago. She has won gold, silver, and bronze medals at national and state competitions. Currently pursuing BCA, she practices yoga to strengthen her body and mind while maintaining a positive lifestyle.

She also conducts workshops and training sessions for beginners and advanced students, focusing on improving flexibility, strength, and mental calmness. Nishu believes in balancing mind and body through consistent yoga practice.
